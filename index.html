How aura makes your contents
life easier

01

Personalized Content Suggestions

AI-powered suggestions help creators generate and refine content ideas without relying on trends.

  • Personalized ideas based on interests and location
  • Suggestions from local events and past uploads
  • AI chat interface to develop and refine ideas
02

Video Guidance
Practice & Ai Coaching

A private practice space where creators record videos and receive structured AI feedback before posting.

  • Feedback on speaking pace, tone, and clarity
  • Guidance on camera presence and posture
  • Facial engagement analysis
  • Environmental feedback on lighting and noise
03

Video Quality Enhancing with AI

Simple tools that improve video quality without requiring editing experience.

  • Lighting and color correction
  • Audio normalization and noise reduction
  • Sharpness and visual clarity adjustments
  • Subtitles and AI voice-over translation
04

Progress & Reflective Feedback

A reflection-based dashboard that tracks improvement and celebrates growth.

  • Track clarity, confidence, and storytelling
  • Monitor consistency and practice progress
  • Skill-based badges for learning milestones
